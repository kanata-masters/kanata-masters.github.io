Play it Again Sports

Play it Again Sports is located at 488 Terry Fox Drive in Kanata. Play it Again buys, sells, and trades a wide variety of equipment - both new and second-hand. They provide exceptional hands-on customer service as well as skate sharpening. The staff are professional, friendly, and very helpful. Please contact fellow player Marc Switalski at 613-599-9937 or access their website at http://www.playitagainsportsottawa.com/ .

Fellow KMHAers are eligible for a 10% Kanata Masters Hockey Association discount to boot.

Alley Cafe

The Alley Cafe is located in the lower level of the KRC and is the place to go to eat the best pizza in town, wings, and to have some cold beverages after a game of hockey at the KRC. Sammy and Nadime are the owners and they are basically KMHA family. Sammy and Nadime also host our year-end banquet and they do not disappoint. They offer delicious take-out pizza as well. For further information please contact them at 613-836-4058.

Kanata Sports Club

The Kanata Sports Club have been a long time sponsor over the years and their contributions to the league and generosity over the years is very much appreciated. They are located on the upper level of the Jack Charron Arena, the Kanata Sports Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting amateur sports and supporting community events. They regularly support local events in Kanata, such as the annual Christmas parade.

See: www.kanatasportsclub.com
