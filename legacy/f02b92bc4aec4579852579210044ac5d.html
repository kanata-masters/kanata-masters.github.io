Code of Conduct

Revised December 17, 2013.

The aim of the Kanata Masters Hockey Association is to provide a safe
friendly environment for our members to play a game they enjoy at an adult
recreational level. We have a responsibility to all members to ensure the
rules of hockey are enforced keeping in mind the varying capabilities of our
members and the reasons we play - exercise, fellowship and enjoyment.
The purpose of our “Code of Conduct” is to:
a) ensure the players are aware of the consequence of their actions
b) ensure the players are aware of the extent the league will go to protect
their members from injury
c) document a minimum standard of discipline that will be followed

Minor Penalties

- accumulation of 3 minor penalties to a player in one game will result
in ejection from that game and one additional game

Ten Minute Misconducts

- first offence and second offence - no further action
- third offence - one additional game suspension
- fourth offence - subject to league review

Game Misconducts

- first offence - no further action unless in the last ten minutes of the game. In this example, a one game suspension will apply
- second offence and third offence - one additional game
- fourth offence - subject to league review

Gross Misconducts

- a gross misconduct could be issued for limited physical or verbal
abuse of officials (including timekeeper) as well as for excessive
behaviour. i.e. touching or bumping an official
- first offence - 3 game suspension
- second offence - expulsion from the league

Please noteï¿½that all misconducts will be reviewed by the disciplinary committee, who may determine that additional action is warranted for exceptional infractions.

- aggressive abuse or verbal threats towards officials will result in an
immediate expulsion from the league

Major Penalty (not including fighting)

- first offence - remainder of the game plus one additional game
- second offence - remainder of the game plus three additional games
- third offence - subject to review by the league and possible expulsion
from the league

Major Penalty (fighting)

- first offence - remainder of the game plus two additional games
- second offence - remainder of the game plus five additional games
- a player identified as the aggressor or instigator will receive one
additional game to the above
- a player identified as the third man into an altercation will be issued a
game misconduct penalty and a minimum of one game suspension in
addition to the above

Match Penalty

- first offence - remainder of the game plus and additional three games
- second offence - subject to review by the league and possible
expulsion from the league

Checking From Behind

- checking from behind anywhere on the ice will result in one of the
following and is subject to further review by the league:
a) a minor penalty and a game misconduct
b) a major penalty and a game misconduct
c) a match penalty and a game misconduct

Accumulation of Penalty Minutes

- Players reaching a plateau of 20 minutes in penalties will receive a
one game
suspension

- Players reaching a plateau of 30 minutes in penalties will receive a
two game suspension

- Players reaching a plateau of 40 minutes in penalties will receive a
three game suspension

NEW Playoffs
All players' penalties will be reset to 0 at the start of the playoffs. The threshold to miss a game due to the amount of penalty minutes is 10 in the playoffs.

Ten-minute misconducts are recorded as
ten minutes and match and game misconducts are recorded as five
minutes.

Discipline Committee

The discipline committee may, at any time, recommend to the league
executive a player's expulsion from the league.

***For disciplinary purposes and to establish game suspensions, major
and match penalties together with gross misconduct infractions in the
preceding and current years will be taken into consideration by the
league.
***Any play that results in the injury to a player may be subject to
further review by the league.
***Following a league review, should the decision be to expel a player,
the reimbursement of fees will be at the discretion of the league.
