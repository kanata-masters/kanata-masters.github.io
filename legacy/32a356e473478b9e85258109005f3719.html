Kanata Masters Hockey Association - News Item

Golf Tournament

Good Afternoon everyone and welcome to the Master's 2nd Annual Golf Classic to be held again at the Mississippi golf & country club. Again we are going to have a great day of fun and companionship with a beautiful day of golf on a great and challenging course. Unlike last year we are only running with 72 golfers so the 1st 72 paid are in to enjoy that day. We are again going to have a 2017 Chevy Equinox sponsored by Chevy West in Kanata to be won for a hole in one. Also there will be all kinds of prizes and trophies to be won by your talents.

So don't miss out Golf Cart,Roast Beef Meal and prizes for just $95.00 to the 1st 72 golfers paid. You can pay by cheque ,master card or visa.

The tournament will be a shotgun start on the 2nd of June at 1:00 pm.Registration starts at 11:00 am.

So to wrap it up the 2nd of June at the Mississippi golf club to the 1st 72 golfers paid.

I will see you all there.
