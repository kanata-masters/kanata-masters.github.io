New Player Registration

Are you a hockey player, fifty years of age or older, looking for a great hockey league to play in?

We are a six team league that plays Wednesday and Friday nights at the Tony Graham Recreation Complex / Kanata Recreation Centre, from the start of September through to the end of March.

It is a fun, mildly competitive, highly social league. There are no slap shots or body checking.

If you are interested, click to email Anthony Hunter, the Kanata Masters President.
